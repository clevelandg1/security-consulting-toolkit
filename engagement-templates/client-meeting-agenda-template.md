### 1. Review Purpose & Meeting Goals

**Time:** 5 minutes
**Led By:** [Consultant Name]

**Objective:** Orient all attendees to today's meeting goals and confirm the agenda.

**Facilitator Actions:**
- Welcome attendees; note any new participants and make introductions
- State today's agenda and confirm timing
- State the primary goal of today's meeting in one sentence
  - *Example: "Today's goal is to review our progress on the remediation action items from last month, surface any new gaps, and introduce a recommendation for improving your SIEM coverage."*
- Invite any immediate agenda additions or priority changes from the Client
- Confirm note-taker and any recording arrangements

**Notes:**

> [Space for meeting-opening notes]

### 2. Current Posture Overview

**Time:** 15 minutes
**Led By:** [Consultant Name] with Client input

**Objective:** Establish a shared, current-state picture of the Client's security posture before diving into specifics.

**Talking Points & Questions to Ask:**

- [ ] **Vulnerability posture:** What does the current vulnerability landscape look like?
  - *"Let's start with a quick look at where things stand today. How did patching go this month? Any surprises in the last scan?"*
  - Share or reference the current vulnerability metrics (total open, critical, high, SLA compliance %)

- [ ] **Recent incidents or alerts:** Any notable security events since last meeting?
  - *"Before we get into the agenda — were there any security incidents, significant alerts, or near-misses since we last spoke that we should be aware of?"*

- [ ] **Environment or business changes:** Anything new in the environment that affects security?
  - *"Any significant changes since last month — new systems, cloud deployments, acquisitions, staff changes, or new applications?"*

- [ ] **Compliance or audit activity:** Any regulatory or audit developments?
  - *"Any updates on the compliance front — audits underway, new requirements, or upcoming deadlines?"*

- [ ] **Morale / momentum check:** How is the team feeling about security progress?
  - *"How is the team feeling overall — do you feel like you're making progress? Anything that's been frustrating or blocking?"*

**Notes:**

> [Space for posture overview notes]
