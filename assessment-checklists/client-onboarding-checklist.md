## How to Use This Checklist

This checklist guides the consulting team through all onboarding activities from pre-engagement setup through ongoing engagement initiation. It follows the Customer Advisory Framework (CAF) baseline assessment approach: discovery-first, structured baselining, and alignment of security activities to business objectives before any remediation work begins.

Complete each item in sequence. Use the Status column to track progress. Flag blockers in the Notes field and escalate to the engagement manager if any Phase 1 or 2 item cannot be completed before the kickoff call.

**Status Options:**
- [ ] Not Started
- [~] In Progress
- [x] Complete
- [B] Blocked — note reason
